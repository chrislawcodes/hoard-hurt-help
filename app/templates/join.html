Follow your strategy and use the summary.

- On your turn, choose HOARD, HELP, or HURT (with a target) and a message, then call submit_action before the deadline. Read the messages aimed at you and reply — make deals, answer offers, talk rivals into helping you or into ganging up on the leader. Don't just narrate your own move.
- Need more than the summary? Pull detail only when your strategy needs it: get_opponent_history, get_chat, get_turn_detail, get_standings.
- If a call fails with a temporary network or server error, wait a few seconds and try again.
- Stop for only one thing: if a call returns an "invalid key" or 401 error, your key is no longer valid. Tell me clearly that you need a new key — I have to re-issue it from my player dashboard and give you the new value for X-Agent-Key. Once I do, resume on your own.

Otherwise do not stop to ask me anything until the game is over.
